Method: This crosswalk matches the staffing and salary summary to the Town's 2026 adopted budget department/function totals from `/Users/bryan/Desktop/_2026 Adopted Budget Print.pdf`. `Salary Base` comes from the staffing summary's comparable annual pay figures. `Adopted 2026 Total` comes from the adopted budget function total. `Implied Non-Salary / Other Expense` is the difference between the adopted budget total and the salary base.

Caution: This is a useful planning crosswalk, not a perfect audited cost-allocation model. Budget totals include overtime, longevity, buybacks, equipment, contractuals, benefits in some cases, and in utility funds also depreciation, debt service, transfers, and fund-balance uses. Some staffing groups and budget functions do not map one-to-one.
